The approved grading scheme under the minimum academic standard is applicable in grading undergraduate degree course in the department of Computer and Communications Engineering. The grading system used in the University is as presented in the table below.

A student shall pass an examination if he/she scores grade of "E" (1.00 Point) or above. A student who absents himself/herself from an examination for a course which he has duly registerded without a reason acceptable to SENATE will automatically earn "F" grade for the examination.
