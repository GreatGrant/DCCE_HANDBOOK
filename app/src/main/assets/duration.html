Degree Nomenclature

The Department runs the Bachelor of Engineering (B. Eng.) degree courses in Computer Engineering and Communications Engineering.

Duration of Course

The Bachelor of Engineering (B. Eng.) course in Computer and Communications Engineering normally last for five (5) academic years (100 to 500 Levels). However, direct entry candidates spend four (4) academic years. The course is structured in such a way that all Engineering students offer the same basic courses at 100 to 300 levels in order to prepare them for the Engineering profession. The 400 and 500 level courses are more detailed and focused towards professional training in Computer and Communications Engineering.

Graduation Requirements

To be eligible for the award of a degree, a student must obtain a minimum total number of courses units specified by the department including those earned from industrial experience and University course requirements.
