OVERALL MANAGEMENT OF THE DEPARTMENT The Department of Computer and Communication Engineering, a full academic Department, is headed by a Coordinator who is the Head of Department. He is appointed by the Vice-Chancellor based on the results of election, every two years, conducted by Academic members of Staff of the Department. The Head of Department is assisted by administrative staff comprising the Departmental Secretary and two (1) office assistants. The Head of Department is also supported by the Academic Staff, Technologists and other Technical Staff as shown in Figure 1. Staff are broadly grouped and developed along two specializations which are: Computer Engineering and Communications Engineering. At the beginning of each session, duties are assigned to members of staff of the Department. All Academic matters are discussed at the meeting of Academic Staff. Decisions and recommendations are forwarded to the faculty board for further input and to Senate for final decision.
